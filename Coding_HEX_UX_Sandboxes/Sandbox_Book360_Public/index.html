About Us.

Book360 is a tour operating software solution, equipped with customisable tools for travel professionals, assisting agents and customers throughout their booking journey.

Using Book360, agents are able to manage all the needs of their client’s, while simultaneously developing their business capacity with ease. The Book360 platform could be the differentiating factor that puts you ahead of other competing operators in an increasingly contested market place.

First Things First

Many legacy booking systems used within the travel sector are outdated and fall short on important features such as document production (itineraries, quotes and travel guides) as well as remote access.

Within an increasingly contested marketplace, systems that are slow and expensive are not viable solutions for travel companies that need to stay ahead of the curve. Book360 addresses the missing document production features or even move away from the legacy systems altogether.
