Tripbuilder 101By Hex Africa

The TripBuilder is your central reservations and booking system

The TripBuilder tool is used by consultants to design TravelGuides from scratch or based upon pre-costed packages.
TripBuilder is part of a larger system called Book360, see below a few of the functions:

  1. TripBuilder
  2. DocBulder
  3. TravelGuide
  4. DashBoard

TripBuilder does the following functions:

  • Design, plan and create itineraries / packages.
  • Enables a travel consultant to check availability and secure reservations.
  • Enables the travel consultant to create quotes for the travel agent (B2B) / traveller (B2C).
  • Powers the TravelGuide mobile app, the TravelGuide web app and the TravelGuide PDF.
  • Enables the ability to create invoices through the DocBuilder.

How to Access the TripBuilder...

Go to https://www.book360.co.za/login(AAA) or https://www.preproduction.book360.co.za/login (Abang) and login to your account.
Create new booking from scratch:

  • Click on Tripbuilder Bookings and then select My Bookings
  • Click on "Create a new booking" and select the "Create a new booking from scratch"
  • Start the booking process by clicking on Dates and select the amount of days for the trip
  • Click on the Edit trip information button, it is located at the top right corner of the landing page.
    Here you must insert the Party name, Agent (click on the drop-down and select the agent) Budget and the Status (click on the drop-down and select the status) of the booking on this window. To save the information you have filled in click on “Save”.

How to Access the TripBuilder

  • Click on "Pax Configuration" button, it is located at the top right corner of the landing page and then the “Add room“ button. Here you must insert the Name of the room, Room type (click on the drop-down and select the room type) and add the amount of adults, children and infants (click on “Add adult”, “Add child” and “Add infant”). To save the information you have filled in click on “Save room details”.
  • After you have selected all the necessary services click on “ Save Booking”.
  • Once you have saved the booking it will generate the “ABFI” reference number and you will have the option to either view the booking summary or continue editing in the TripBuilder.

Accommodation

Accommodation is a room, a group of rooms, or a building in which someone may live or stay.

  • Destination - click on the dropdown and select the area in which the accommodation is situated.E.g. Botswana.
  • Supplier - type in the name of the accommodation. E.g. Holiday Inn Express.
  • Address - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Room type - the room type will automatically be inserted once you have chosen the accommodation.
  • Check in & out - select the check out and check in dates from the calendar.

Excursion

An excursion is a short journey or trip, especially one taken as a leisure activity.

  • Destination - click on the dropdown and select the area in which the excursion is situated. E.g. Cape Town CBD
  • Supplier - type in the name of the excursion.E.g. City Sightseeing Cape Town.
  • Address - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Excursion type - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Pick up & Drop off - select the check out and check in dates from the calendar. At the bottom of the calendar you will find the dropdown to select the time for the pick up and drop off.

Meet and Greet

A Meet & Greet, is usually in the arrivals hall at the airport, where the driver meets the traveller(s) and assist them with their luggage and answers any questions they may have, then the driver will transfer them to their accommodation.

  • Destination - click on the dropdown and select the area in which the driver has to collect the traveller.E.g. Gauteng Pretoria.
  • Supplier - type in the name of the company that will be meeting the traveller at the location.E.g. African Ample Assistance.
  • Physical address - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Service description - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Pick up time - select the pick up date from the calendar. At the bottom of the calendar you will find the dropdown to select the time for the pick up.

Car Rental

A car rental is a company that rents out vehicles for a short period of time for a certain fee depending on the category and class of the vehicle, as well as the amount of days the vehicle will be out for rental. There are also multiple insurances that can be taken out while the vehicle is in the possession of the customer.

  • Supplier - type in the name of the company you would like to rent a car from. E.g. Bidvest.
  • Pick up location - the place where the traveller has to pick up the car. E.g. BedFordView Eastgate.
  • Drop off location - the place where the traveller has to drop off the car.E.g. Claremont, Cavendish Square.
  • Service description - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Pick up time - select the pick up date from the calendar. At the bottom of the calendar you will find the dropdown to select the pick up time.
  • Drop off time - select the drop off date from the calendar. At the bottom of the calendar you will find the dropdown to select the drop off time.

Transfer

A transfer is when the traveller meets the driver at the arrivals / info desk at the airport or at the pick-up zone outside of the airport. Travellers find their own way from the arrivals hall to the transfer.

  • Destination - click on the dropdown and select the area in which the driver has to collect the traveller.E.g. Free State.
  • Supplier - the name of the company that will collect the traveller from the destination.
  • Physical address - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Service description - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Pick up time - select the pick up date from the calendar. At the bottom of the calendar you will find the dropdown to select the pick up time.

Entrance Fee

An entrance fee is a sum of money which you pay before you go somewhere such as a cinema or museum, or which you have to pay in order to join an organization or institution.

  • Destination - click on the dropdown and select the area in which the traveller will need to pay a fee.E.g. Bloemfontein.
  • Supplier - the name of the where the traveller has to pay a fee.E.g. Parking fee at Parliament Hotel.
  • Physical address - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Service description - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Pick up time - select the pick up date from the calendar.

Flight

A flight is the action or process of flying through the air with an aeroplane / airplane.

  • Supplier - the name of the airline the traveller will be using.
  • Departure - the city where the traveller will be departing.
  • Arrival - the city where the traveller will be arriving.
  • Service description - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Departure Time
  • Arrival Time

Meal

A meal is the food served and eaten at regular occasions for taking food during the day such as breakfast, lunch, and supper.

  • Destination - click on the dropdown and select the area in which the driver has to collect the traveller.E.g. Cape Boland.
  • Supplier - the name of the establishment where the traveller will be having the meal.E.g. Capetonian Hotel.
  • Physical address - will automatically be inserted by the system.
  • Service description - will automatically me inserted by the system.
  • Meal time - select the date and time on the calendar.

Create a new booking from a package:

  • Click on “TripBuilder Bookings” and then select “My Bookings”.
  • Click on “Create a new booking” and select the “Create a new booking from a package”.
  • Start the booking process by selecting a package from the grid.
  • Fill in the traveller(s) name and select the agent from the dropdown.
  • Click on “Create new booking” to proceed with the booking.
  • Once you have clicked on “Create new booking”, a window will pop up with the “ABFI” reference number for the booking. You will also have the option to either view the booking or continue to edit.
